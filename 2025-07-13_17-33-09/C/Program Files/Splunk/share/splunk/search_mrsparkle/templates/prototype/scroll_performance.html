Notes

  • Any page with fixed positioning where the fixed content overlaps static content (and/or vice versa) scrolling will be rough
  • Replacing "pre.eventRaw span.srch" with "pre.eventRaw span" shaves ~10-20 milliseconds on initial render.
  • Replacing whitespace with &nbsp; has a negative impact on initial render.
